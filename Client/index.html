Foundational Vision

Established in 1989, Rada Constructions was born out of a desire to improve lives through thoughtful and reliable construction. Over the years, the firm has remained committed to turning aspirations into reality, combining practical engineering with a clear sense of purpose and care in every project.

Guiding Force

Mr. Masood Ahmed, a Civil Engineering graduate from the renowned Jabalpur Engineering College (1983), has always believed in building more than just structures. His leadership, rooted in integrity and foresight, has guided the firm's journey for over three decades. He remains the cornerstone of Rada's values, inspiring a culture of trust, resilience, and enduring quality.

Global Experience

With six years of valuable experience working on large-scale projects in Saudi Arabia, Mr. Ahmed returned to India equipped with a global perspective. Recognized Excellence

Mr. Ahmed's unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him several prestigious honors, including the Glory of India Award and the Bharat Jyoti Award. These accolades reflect not only his contributions to engineering but also his dedication to building a better, stronger nation through his work.
